Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of plants do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of plants including flowers, shrubs, trees, succulents, and herbs. Whether you're looking for ornamental plants to beautify your garden or herbs for your kitchen, we have something for everyone.

What is your return policy?

We have a no refund or return policy on live plant material. Our plants are healthy when leaving the Nursery.

Can you advise me on landscaping and plant arrangement for my garden?

Absolutely! Our team of experienced horticulturists can provide personalized landscaping advice tailored to your garden's specific conditions and your preferences. Whether you're aiming for a vibrant flower bed or a low-maintenance garden, we're here to help you achieve your vision.

How do I know if a plant is suitable for the climate in my area?

Our nursery specializes in plants that are well-suited to the local climate. However, if you're unsure about a specific plant's suitability for your area, feel free to ask our staff for guidance. We can recommend plants that are known to thrive in your particular climate zone and provide tips for ensuring their success in your garden.
